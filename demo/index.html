A Multi-Faceted Tool That

Benefits Many Types Of Users

Players

PlayerFax™ is valuable tool that offers a means to become the best they can be in the sports they love, tracking milestones and providing a way to target specific goals. Scores and stats are tracked across multiple sports and multiple seasons, demonstrating benchmarks that can be used to refine skills and set new goals. By using PlayerFax™, student athletes have a clear picture of their standings as a player and a roadmap to forge a path to the college track.

COACHES

PlayerFax™ is a means to manage their team more efficiently and better utilize their student athletes. Coaches can review performance stats from every game, track progress and see trends that can help them identify what needs to be addressed during practices. They can provide additional support and encouragement, helping to hone student athletes to realize their potential.

EVENT COORDINATORS

With a touch of a button, notify players of local events, keep track of game results and share information with parents, coaches and student athletes.

PlayerFax makes it easy to advertise events and support local sports within the community.

PARENTS

The eternal question of “How is my child progressing” is answered with quantifiable data, not a coach’s polite response. Supportive parents can help guide their student athletes more effectively and see what areas they can provide extra support and encouragement. Parents stay in the loop and have a means to discuss their student’s progress, help with goal setting and keep in touch with an important part of their child’s life.

RECRUITERS

The PlayerFax transcript can be an indispensable resource for locating talented student athletes and refining searches for college-track players.

Recruiters will have a clear, decisively accurate picture of a student athlete’s performance at a glance.

Fans

Friends, family and fans can connect with their favorite local teams and players, review scores and stats, and stay in the loop with upcoming sporting events.

Social sharing features allow fans to post information and invite others to local games to support their favorite teams. Get up-to-the-minute news about conferences and other great spectator events while supporting your top picks.

PlayerFax™ provides a means to the progress of players, giving them a transcript that can potentially be used to attract college recruiters down the road. Much like academic transcripts, the PlayerFax™ data measures players’ aptitudes with standardized competitive scoring. For passionate student athletes, PlayerFax™ can provide opportunities by recording key moments and demonstrating the strengths, dedication and potential of college track athletes.

We make uploading player stats and keeping track of progress simple with a user-friendly interface. PlayerFax™ aims to standardize and centralize comparative scoring by age for school and club players across multiple sports. And best of all, PlayerFax™ is free to use!
